About me

Hi, I am Wei Zhen. I have worked in AI research and engineering since 2018.

I am interested in how to make generative models learn and simulate real-world processes: trajectories and interactions, multimodal signals, and agentic workflows. I also care about methods that make these systems controllable and useful under practical deployment and operation constraints.

I started my career at Lyrebird in Montreal (later acquired by Descript), where I worked on neural speech synthesis, voice cloning, and AI speech editing. I moved to Singapore in 2021 and worked on eKYC computer vision problems at Advance AI. I led AI development for document verification technology used in production across Southeast Asia. I later joined Motion G, where I built LLM agents and systems for industrial motion-control engineering workflows and business processes.

I am currently working independently on controllable generative modeling for sequential data. I am also exploring applied research opportunities where I can work on generative AI and world models.

You can find more of my work on X, GitHub, and Google Scholar.
